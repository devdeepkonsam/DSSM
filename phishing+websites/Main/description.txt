This dataset contains information about phishing websites, with a total of 11,550 data instances and 30 features. 
Each instance represents a website, and each feature describes a specific characteristic that may indicate whether a website is legitimate or phishing.

Features include:
- having_IP_Address
- URL_Length
- Shortining_Service
- having_At_Symbol
- double_slash_redirecting
- Prefix_Suffix
- having_Sub_Domain
- SSLfinal_State
- Domain_registeration_length
- Favicon
- port
- HTTPS_token
- Request_URL
- URL_of_Anchor
- Links_in_tags
- SFH

- Abnormal_URL
- Redirect
- on_mouseover

- popUpWidnow
- Iframe
- age_of_domain
- DNSRecord
- web_traffic
- Page_Rank
- Google_Index
- Links_pointing_to_page
- Statistical_report
- Result

The dataset is used for machine learning and data analysis tasks to detect phishing websites based on these features.

-1: Indicates a phishy or abnormal condition for that feature, signaling a negative indicator of legitimacy.

0: Indicates a borderline or uncertain condition where the signal is suspicious but not definitively phishy or legitimate.

1: Indicates a legitimate or normal condition for that feature, signaling a positive indicator of legitimacy.
